4.3 iframes and accessibility pitfalls

Beware of navigation traps (including too much info) + Research this!

4.4 General tips for iframes

Here are a few tips about embedding content:

  • don’t have too many iframes per page (can be slow to load, take up resources)
  • student interactions aren’t persistent: they get reloaded by a student every time they visit a page
  • Consider mobile/tablet users where the screen sizes are smaller: sometimes a link is better?
  • Avoid sites with lots of advertising or a cookie requests that distracts from the content.
  • Add a title to make it clear to screen readers or if content doesn’t load
